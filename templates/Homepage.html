Our Volunteerism Mission

At PSU, we believe that academic excellence is incomplete without social responsibility. Our mission is to foster a culture of service that empowers students to lead, innovate, and contribute to the socio-economic development of Pangasinan and beyond through structured, impactful volunteer opportunities.
